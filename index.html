Profile

Hi, my name is Vladi Angelov, a Bulgarian / British citizen living in Crawley, West Sussex.

I have a Master's degree in Geophysics and for nearly 9 years I worked for the largest company in this field - CGG. Starting as a junior scientist and becoming a team leader in a few years, I am an expert in data processing, analysis and data science.

Although I love geoscience, throughout my career I enjoyed programming the most and in 2019 I took some time to grow my expertise in Web Development and Computer Science, by taking a 6 month intensive coding bootcamp at Le Wagon, followed by another 6 month Computer Science for Web Development course by Harvard University.

Since 2020 I am part of the product team of Sleepstation, working as a Full Stack Developer. My core tech stack is Ruby on Rails and React, however I do have some experience with Django and Flask too. I have recently become interested in Functional Programming and Ramda too.

I am also quite confident with building and using REST APIs, have significant experience working with GraphQL and years of experience with HTML, CSS/SCSS, SQL, vanilla JavaScript and Bootstrap to mention a few.
